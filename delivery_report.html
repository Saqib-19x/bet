4. Quick Usage Guide

For Players

  1. Sign up / Log in — create an account or sign in from the welcome screen.
  2. Add funds — go to Wallet → Deposit and top up via UPI / payment method.
  3. Find a match — open Sports or Home and select an event.
  4. Place a bet — on the match screen, click a Back (blue) or Lay (pink) price. The Place Bet panel opens — choose a quick-stake chip or type a stake, check the profit / liability, and press Submit.
  5. Combine selections — add two or more picks to the Bet Slip to create a multi-leg bet at combined odds.
  6. Track & withdraw — follow bets under My Bets; withdraw winnings from Wallet → Withdraw.

For Administrators

  1. Open the Admin Panel — admin accounts see an Admin Panel link (hidden from players).
  2. Monitor the business — the Dashboard shows users, active bets, revenue and pending withdrawals; Risk shows live exposure and worst-case liability per match.
  3. Manage markets — under Odds & Markets, add markets, set the stream, post live commentary, and suspend or settle markets.
  4. Manage users — search users, adjust balances, and suspend / block accounts under Users.
  5. Settle bets — review and settle results under Bets.
  6. Approve payments — approve or reject deposits and withdrawals under Transactions.
